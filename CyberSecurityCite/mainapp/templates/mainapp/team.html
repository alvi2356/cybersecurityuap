Our Convener

Dr. Muhammad Towfiqur Rahman

We believe that cyber activities or cyber security abilities are really crucial at this time. As a result, the CSE department at UAP has established a Cyber Security Club to improve cyber security awareness. I believe it will assist students in pursuing a rewarding career in the fields of cryptography, forensics, incident response, and secure network management. Students will be trained to increase their technical knowledge and abilities in order to take on the world's security challenges through this Club.
